Ushelf

Ushelf will serve as a platform where individuals can organize their digital books in a personalized way. Users can invite others to view their shelves using a unique Ushelf username and ID. The platform will also feature a calendar and email notifications for managing reading schedules..

Tips on How to Use UShelf:

  • Use the "My Calendar" to schedule and manage your events.
  • Add important items to "My Shelf" to keep track of books, movies, tasks, and more.
  • Visit "About Us" to learn more about our mission and team.
